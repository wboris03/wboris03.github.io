Research themes and directions

Geometric Optimal Control

Zermelo-type problems, first- and second-order optimality conditions, and geometric aspects related to Finsler and Riemannian structures.

Quantum Optimal Control

Structure-preserving numerical schemes, Cayley-transform approaches, and optimization algorithms for linear and nonlinear quantum systems.

PDE-Constrained Optimal Control

Optimal control of mean curvature flows and related PDE systems, including finite element discretization and computational implementation.

Turnpike Theory

Continuous- and discrete-time turnpike phenomena, especially for systems with symmetries and reduced dynamics.

Numerical Algorithms

Direct and indirect optimal control methods, reversible integrators, and computational frameworks for large-scale control problems.
