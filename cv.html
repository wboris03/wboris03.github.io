Selected Research Achievements

Numerical Methods for Quantum Optimal Control

Development of higher-order numerical methods based on the Cayley transform for quantum systems, with the aim of improving efficiency and convergence in quantum optimal control.

Turnpike Theory in Optimal Control

Contributions to the analysis of turnpike phenomena, including links with singular perturbation theory and extensions of exponential turnpike results to optimal control problems with symmetries.

Zermelo Navigation Problems and Finsler Geometry

Study of abnormal geodesics, optimal synthesis, conjugate and cut loci, and geometric classification of geodesic flows for Zermelo-type problems on surfaces of revolution.
