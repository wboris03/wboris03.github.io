About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in optimal control and applications at Paderborn University, Germany. Since July 2022, I have been a member of the Numerik und Steuerung group, led by Prof. Dr. Sina Ober-Blöbaum at the Institute of Mathematics.

Previously, from December 2021 to July 2022, I served as Attaché Temporaire d’Enseignement et de Recherche (ATER) at Toulouse INP-ENSEEIHT, France. From October 2018 to November 2021, I was a doctoral researcher at Université Toulouse III – Paul Sabatier, where I conducted my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Joseph Gergaud and Dr. Olivier Cots.

Earlier in 2018, I carried out a post-master research internship at Toulouse INP-ENSEEIHT, also under the supervision of Prof. Joseph Gergaud and Dr. Olivier Cots.

My research focuses on optimal control theory and its applications to complex dynamical systems, combining analytical and numerical perspectives. I am particularly interested in geometric optimal control, quantum optimal control, PDE-constrained optimal control, turnpike theory for systems with symmetries, and the design of efficient numerical algorithms based on direct and indirect methods.
