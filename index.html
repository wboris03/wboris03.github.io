Applied Mathematics · Optimal Control · Numerical Analysis

Research in optimal control across geometry, quantum systems, PDEs, and numerical methods.

I am a post-doctoral researcher at Paderborn University. My work develops analytical and computational approaches for optimal control problems arising in geometric mechanics, quantum dynamics, PDE-constrained systems, and symmetry-driven control theory.

View Publications Open CV Contact

Research Areas

  • Geometric optimal control and Zermelo-type problems
  • Quantum optimal control and Cayley-based numerical methods
  • Optimal control of PDEs and mean curvature flows
  • Turnpike theory for symmetric control systems
  • Direct and indirect numerical algorithms for optimal control
